# ☀️ SunCart — Your Summer Essentials Sanctuary 🍦

Welcome to **SunCart**, an eCommerce experience where the sun never sets! 🌊 This project is a modern, high-end summer store built for the **category-A8-Jackfruit** assignment. It features a "cozy-web" aesthetic with soft textures, intentional motion, and a warm, inviting atmosphere.

---

## ✨ Project Overview
**SunCart** is designed to make summer shopping feel like a breeze. From stylish sunglasses to beach essentials, users can browse a curated collection of seasonal favorites. With a focus on unique design and seamless authentication, SunCart ensures your journey from browsing to checkout is as smooth as a summer sunset.

*   **Live URL:** [https://suncart-store-wine.vercel.app/](https://suncart-store-wine.vercel.app/)
*   **Developer:** Mst. Gulnahar🌸

---

## 🎀 Key Features

*   **🌅 Dreamy Home Page:** Featuring a vibrant Hero Section with "Hot Deals 🔥" and a "Popular Products" showcase.
*   **🔒 Secure Details:** A Protected Product Details route that keeps our best items exclusive to members.
*   **🔐 Seamless Auth:** Powered by BetterAuth, featuring smooth Email/Password login and a "One-Tap" Google Handshake.
*   **📱 Fluidly Responsive:** Hand-crafted to look gorgeous on Mobile, Tablet, and Desktop.
*   **👤 Profile & Update:** A dedicated space for users to manage their identity (Bonus Feature! 🌟).
*   **🍹 Summer Care Tips:** Extra sections with hydration and skincare advice for those sunny days.

---

## 🛠️ The Tech Stack

This project utilizes a high-end frontend stack to achieve its unique look and feel:

*   **Framework:** Next.js (App Router).
*   **Styling:** Tailwind CSS & Lucide Icons.
*   **Authentication:** BetterAuth (Email & Google Provider).
*   **Database Integration:** Neon DB and Prisma.
*   **Animations:** Framer Motion & Lottie.
*   **Deployment:** Vercel.
